# Product

## Register

product

## Users
QA Engineers, Developers, and Product Managers who need to rapidly generate test cases and E2E automation from requirements or existing Jira tickets.

## Product Purpose
To serve as a complete toolkit for modern QA by automating the creation of test plans, manual test cases, Playwright scripts, and visual bug reporting, reducing manual QA overhead.

## Brand Personality
Precise, modern, technical, and efficient.

## Anti-references
- Clunky, high-density 2000s enterprise testing dashboards (e.g., legacy ALM tools).
- Generic AI "purple/cyan glow" landing pages with infinite marquees.
- Falsely complex tech-bro bento grids that lack rhythm.

## Design Principles
- **Speed is a feature:** Interactions must feel instantaneous (< 300ms).
- **Function over decoration:** Data density should serve the workflow; avoid decorative slop.
- **Clear affordances:** Buttons should feel tactile (`scale(0.98)` on active) and states (loading, empty) must be deliberately designed.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA minimum contrast for all text, forms, and interactive elements.
- Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` for all animations.
